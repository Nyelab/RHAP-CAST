RIVER HERRING AVOIDANCE PROGRAM FORECAST

The River Herring Avoidance Program Forecast (RHAPCAST) forecasts regions to maximize encounters of target species (Atlantic herring or Atlantic mackerel) while minimizing river herring bycatch. Forecasts are provided one week in advance to help you plan. Look for a new forecast each Monday!
