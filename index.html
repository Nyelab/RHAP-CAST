RIVER HERRING AVOIDANCE PROGRAM FORECAST

**River herring** are truly remarkable fish! These anadromous wonders undertake incredible, arduous journeys from the ocean back to freshwater to spawn, playing a **vital ecological role** in coastal ecosystems. Their populations face unique challenges, and the River Herring Avoidance Program Forecast (RHAPCAST) aims to help resource managers and the public make informed decisions to protect them.
